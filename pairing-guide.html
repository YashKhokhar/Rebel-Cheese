The Ultimate Vegan Cheese Pairing Guide

Elevate your plant-based cheese experience with perfect combinations

Creating the perfect vegan cheese board is an art form that combines rich, dairy-free flavors with complementary pairings of wine, fruits, and crackers. Whether you're hosting a dinner party or enjoying a quiet night in, these pairing suggestions will help you create memorable flavor combinations.

Vegan Cheese and Wine Pairings

Aged Cheeses with Red Wine

Pair our aged vegan cheeses like Sharp Cheddar or Smoked Gouda with full-bodied red wines such as Cabernet Sauvignon or Malbec. The bold flavors complement each other perfectly.

Soft Cheeses with White Wine

Our creamy soft cheeses pair beautifully with crisp white wines like Sauvignon Blanc or Chardonnay. The acidity cuts through the richness of the cheese.

Fruit Pairings for Vegan Cheese

Berries

Fresh strawberries, raspberries, and blackberries pair wonderfully with our creamy soft cheeses, adding a sweet-tart contrast.

Pears and Apples

The sweetness of sliced pears and apples complements our aged cheeses beautifully, especially when paired with nuts.

Grapes

A classic pairing that works with all our cheeses, grapes provide a juicy, refreshing contrast to rich flavors.

Cracker and Bread Pairings

Whole Grain Crackers

Perfect with our aged cheeses, these add a nutty, earthy flavor that complements the cheese's complexity.

Baguette Slices

The neutral flavor and crisp texture make baguette slices ideal for our soft cheeses and spreads.

Gluten-Free Options

Seed-based crackers provide great texture and flavor that won't overpower our delicate cheese varieties.

Building the Perfect Vegan Cheese Board

1. Select Your Cheeses

Choose 3-5 varieties including aged, soft, and flavored options for variety. Aim for different textures and flavors.

2. Add Accompaniments

Include fresh and dried fruits, nuts, olives, and quality crackers or bread. Add condiments like chutney or honey.

3. Arrange Beautifully

Use different levels and groupings for visual appeal. Place cheeses first, then fill in with other items. Garnish with fresh herbs.
